Chairman's Message

This is an exciting time in healthcare and nursing in particular. Nurses carry on an awesome responsibility to improve the health outcomes of patients and their families. To deliver quality professionalism, nurses have to be professionally well-prepared and trained. And, at Amazan Nursing College, it is this excellent preparation that we offer.

AMAZAN NURSING COLLEGE was established in 2017 with a mission to become a premier provider of nursing education of International standards and emerge as a centre that is recognized for excellence in education, service, research and leadership with a commitment to Bangladesh Government core values.

Md. Rashed Mostafizur
Rahman Founder & Chairman
Amazan Nursing College

Managing Director's Message

Dear Students, Faculty, and Staff of this Nursing College,

I am pleased to address you all today as the Managing Director of this esteemed institution.Our college has a legacy of excellence in nursing education. Amazan Nurisng College is a renowned private residential educational institute established in 2017.

Currently we are running 3 years Diploma in Nursing Science & Midwifery and 3 years Diploma in Midwifery program. As we move forward, we must strive to maintain the high standards that we have set for ourselves. Our commitment to providing quality education and training must remain unwavering, and we must continue to adapt to the changing needs of the healthcare industry.

It is a great time to consider starting or advancing your career in nursing. Prime Minister of Bangladesh Sheikh Hasina has said as she urged more youths to make a career in nursing. She quoted – “It is a noble profession, that’s what I will tell our young generations. They can join this job and emerge as Florence Nightingale.”

Md. Mottakin Hossain (Pranto)
Managing Director
Amazan Nursing College
